Privacy Policy – Stotra Mitra

Last updated: February 2025 | Developer: Your Mate Apps

Your Mate Apps ("we") built Stotra Mitra as a free app. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use our app.

Information Collection and Use

Stotra Mitra does not collect any personal data from you. We use the following third-party services that may collect anonymized data:

Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in the app. AdMob may collect:

  • Device identifiers (e.g., advertising ID)
  • Anonymized usage data for ad personalization

You can learn more about AdMob's privacy practices at Google Privacy Policy.

Firebase Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with the app. This helps us improve the app experience. Firebase Analytics collects anonymized usage data such as:

  • App opens and screen views
  • General device information (e.g., device type, OS version)

No personally identifiable information is collected. See Firebase Privacy for details.

No Other Data Collection

Beyond AdMob and Firebase Analytics as described above, we do not collect, store, or share any personal data. We do not require account creation or any form of registration.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at yourmateapps@gmail.com or visit our website at yourmateapps.github.io.

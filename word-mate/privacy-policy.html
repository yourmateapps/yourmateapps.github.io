Privacy Policy – Word Mate

Effective Date: January 2026 | Developer: Your Mate Apps

Word Mate ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains how information is handled when you use the Word Mate application.

1. Dictionary and Game Data

Dictionary lookups and Hangman game data are processed locally. We do not collect, store, or transmit the words you search for or the content of your gameplay.

2. Advertising

Word Mate displays ads through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers (such as advertising ID), usage data, and other information according to its own privacy policy.

3. Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics to understand how the app is used and to improve the experience. This service collects anonymized data such as app opens, screen views, and general device information. No personally identifiable information is collected. See Firebase Privacy for details.

4. Data Usage

We do not sell personal information. Any data collected by third-party services is used only to deliver app functionality and improve performance. We do not require account creation or registration.

5. Third-Party Services

Word Mate may use third-party services such as Google Play Services, AdMob, and Firebase, each of which operates under its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review their policies.

6. Children's Privacy

Word Mate does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can address it.

7. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at yourmateapps@gmail.com or visit yourmateapps.github.io.

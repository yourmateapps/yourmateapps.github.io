Delete Account – RetroPal

If you signed in using Google within RetroPal and wish to delete your account data:

  1. Open RetroPal
  2. Go to Settings → Google Account
  3. Tap "Sign Out"
  4. Delete cloud backups from your Google Drive (RetroPal folder)
  5. Optionally uninstall the app to remove local data

Alternatively, you may revoke RetroPal access from your Google Account:
https://myaccount.google.com/permissions

Data Deleted:

  • Google Sign-In session
  • Google Drive backup files (if removed by user)
  • Local app data (if app is uninstalled)

We do not maintain user accounts on our own servers.
